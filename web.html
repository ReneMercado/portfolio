The Chicago public art map and database is an interactive tool that highlights the art we see everyday in the street, from the ephemeral to the concrete. Moodsic

Moodsicclose

Moodsic is a mood enhancing app using Music playlists from the Deezer API, and weather from Dark Sky API to generate the backgrounds. Choose from 5 moods: angry, sad, bored, sleepy, romantic, or happy and we'll supply you with the playlist to get you there. We designed the app with accessibility in mind and achieved full CUD compliance, and partial AA compliance. You'll need a free Deezer account to fully utilize the app.
