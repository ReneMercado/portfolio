livingRoom

I was asked by Martha Bayne and Andrea Jablonski to make an installation for
A Memory Palace of fear, a haunted house about housing put together by Theater Oobleck. I based this installation off of the Hans Christian Anderson story The Little Match Girl. Patrons would walk down a hallway and peer through different windows at holiday scenes. At the end of the hallway they would end up in a living room where I provided mixtapes, and food that reminded people of home, drawing materials, paper, and the history of the top 10 most common houses in Chicago and prompted people to draw what home means to them. A Memory Palace of Fear took place from October 19 - October 31, 2017.
